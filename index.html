The Studio

Where craft
meets vision

OAK is a boutique architecture and interior design studio built on the belief that space shapes experience. We work from the ground up — conceiving buildings and interiors as a unified whole, where structure, light, and material serve a singular vision.

From private residences to hospitality environments, every project is a collaboration grounded in rigorous craft, purposeful restraint, and an unwavering attention to the way people move through and inhabit the spaces we create.

What We Do

Design as
discipline

From city-scale master planning to bespoke interior finishing, OAK leads every project from first sketch to final finish — architecture, urban design, and interiors as one inseparable practice.

01

Architecture

Full-service architectural design for residential, commercial, and hospitality projects — from concept and planning through construction administration.

⬡

02

Urban Design

Strategic design at the scale of the city — master planning, public realm, mixed-use districts, and transit-oriented development that shapes how communities grow and gather.

◻

03

Interior Design

Bespoke interior environments conceived as extensions of the architecture — where spatial flow, light, and material tell a coherent story.

◈

04

Spatial Planning

Rigorous spatial analysis and programming that optimizes how people move through and experience every square foot of a building or interior.

◎

05

Material Curation

We source and specify materials with the precision of a craftsperson — considering tactility, durability, sustainability, and aesthetic harmony at every scale.

⬜

06

FF&E Procurement

End-to-end furniture, fixture, and equipment specification and procurement — from one-of-a-kind custom pieces to carefully vetted maker relationships.

◇

07

Project Management

We manage contractors, timelines, and budgets with the same attention we bring to design — ensuring the built result matches the vision without compromise.
